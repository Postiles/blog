Postiles

Ideas For Linking

Postile is a new way for people to link to each other. It is based on topic, common background or interests. In postiles, linking is achieved exceptionly by physically closeness, leaving more freedom and feasibility for users to be creative.

Topic-Based

A place of getting involved in your topics.

Postiles is topic based. Each topic is presented as a board. You could post related information in format of text, image, etc.

You could also invite other users to join the discussion and sharing though our message system.

Constructive

A place of collaboration and building up.

Discussion in Postiles is contructive. It encourages discussion and exchange of ideas. You can initiate a thread anywhere you want and let it to be developped to a direction that could give you great surprise.

Contribute to your group and topic. Enjoy the rest.

Free Style

A place of using your imagination.

Imagine that you now have a scratch paper on the screen. You can create new ways to present your ideas on a board by arranging spaces and posts collaboratively.

Your imagination is the only limit with those square posts.
