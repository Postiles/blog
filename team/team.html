Who We Are

We are a group of passionate students from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. We hold the simple belief that there exists a way to share things freely online. We love our mission and our team. We’ve been working on this since 2012 with the inspiration from a graduation event and finally able to turn the idea into postiles.com. We hope you enjoy the site!
