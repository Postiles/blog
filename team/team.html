Who We Are

We are a group of passionate students from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, with four majored in engineers and one majored in business. The idea of building up this website came from a graduation event. Although all of our team members were not specialist in building up large scale website like this nor specialists in defining UI and UX, being positive and insistent, we manage to achieve some outcomes. Here we proudly present our team!
